Urgent Challenge

Ukraine has 360,000 war-injured individuals, but only 30 centers serving 27,000 patients annually. At current capacity, each person would wait 13.5 years for treatment.

Our Treatment Programs:

  • Limb amputation recovery & prosthetics
  • Traumatic brain injury rehabilitation
  • PTSD and psychological support
  • Musculoskeletal injury treatment
  • Post-stroke rehabilitation

Your donation directly supports patient rehabilitation, keeping our center free for those who need it most. Every contribution helps someone walk again, regain independence, or find hope after trauma.
